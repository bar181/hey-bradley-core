How we work, who we serve, and what to expect

We work primarily with mid-market and enterprise companies across 14 industries — from $50M revenue businesses to Fortune 500s. Our sweet spot is companies experiencing rapid growth, operational complexity, or strategic inflection points where senior outside perspective creates disproportionate value.

Engagements range from focused 6-week diagnostic sprints to 12–18 month transformation partnerships. We scope each engagement to the problem, not a fixed timeline. Most clients return for subsequent engagements — 94% retention speaks to how we structure for lasting impact rather than dependency.

Three things: senior-only delivery (you meet the team you hire), a bias toward implementation over decks, and a track record of $2.4B in client revenue impact. We don't staff projects with juniors. Every engagement is led by a partner with 15+ years of operating experience in your sector.

It's a genuine 30-minute conversation with a senior partner — not a sales pitch. We'll discuss your specific challenges, share relevant examples from our work, and give you at least one actionable insight you can use immediately. No commitment required. If there's a fit, we'll propose a scoped engagement. If not, we'll point you in the right direction.
