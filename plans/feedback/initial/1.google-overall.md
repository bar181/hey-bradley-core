### 3. Fixing "Expert Mode" Enterprise Density 
Your current Expert Mode mockup (from the previous batch) used a consumer-friendly, spacious design. "Enterprise Density" means packing as much actionable data onto the screen as possible without it looking cluttered. Engineers hate scrolling; they want to see the whole state at a glance.

Here is the exact CSS/UX shift you need to feed your Swarm to fix the Expert Mode density:

**A. Typography & Spacing (The "VS Code" Treatment)**
*   **Current:** `text-sm` (14px) or `text-base` (16px) with `gap-6` (24px padding). 
*   **New Density:** Switch to `text-xs` (12px) for all labels. Use `font-mono` (JetBrains Mono or Fira Code) for *all* input values (hex codes, pixel amounts, JSON keys). 
*   **Spacing:** Reduce padding between rows to `gap-1` (4px) or `gap-2` (8px). 

**B. Property Row Layout**
Instead of stacking labels on top of inputs, put them on the same line.
*   *Before:* 
    Background 
    `[ #faf8f5 ]`
*   *Dense:* 
    `Background  ........[ #faf8f5 ]` *(Label on left, input pushed to right edge)*
