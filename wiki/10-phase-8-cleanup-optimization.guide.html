Key Takeaway

Phase 8 proved two things. First: cleanup IS development. Reorganizing 165+ files, fixing 30+ stale references, and consolidating 22 ADRs made the codebase navigable and maintainable for the first time. Second: the spec generators transformed the product narrative from “builder with specs” to “specification platform with preview.” Going from 2 inline generators (B+/B−) to 6 modular pure-function generators is the architectural change that makes capstone-demo-ready real.

⚡ 6 Sprints Delivered

Every sprint completed in a single day across 4 sessions with parallel agent execution.

Sprint 1 — P0

Spec Generators

6 enterprise-quality spec generators as pure functions in src/lib/specGenerators/. Shared helpers with NO truncation, NO slice limits. XAIDocsTab upgraded from 2-button toggle to 6 sub-tabs. AISP grade: B− → A.

Sprint 2 — P1

Plans Cleanup

3-loop optimization with 18 audit agents. Renamed level-1 → phase-1. Archived levels 2–7. Consolidated 22 ADRs into docs/adr/. Fixed typos, 30+ stale path references, removed empty directories.

Sprint 3 — P0

Image/Video Library

208 images across 10 categories with LLM-ready metadata (tags, mood, description, ai_prompt_context). 41 videos across 7 categories. 8 image effect presets.

Sprint 4 — P0

Sprint 5

designLocked toggle in uiStore with TopBar lock button. ThemeSimple disabled when locked. 22 debug console statements removed—zero remaining.

Sprint 6

Integration + Wiring

Onboarding shows 8 example cards. ChatInput has example dropdown. ImagePicker rewritten to use JSON library (208 images, 41 videos, 8 effects). 8 CSS effect animations in index.css.
