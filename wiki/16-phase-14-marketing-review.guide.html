∴ AISP Generator Fix SHIPPED

The AISP generator was producing structured prose with @aisp 2.0 tags instead of formal Crystal Atom notation. This was the highest-priority fix in Phase 14.

What Changed

  • 5 formal Gamma rules — section rendering, enabled gates, toggle constraints, variant constraints, palette binding
  • Crystal Atom format — proper mathematical notation with Omega, Sigma, Gamma, Lambda, Epsilon blocks
  • RAW AISP removed — no more @aisp format in Expert panel editors; AISP lives only in Blueprints tab
  • Naming corrected — "AI Symbolic Protocol" and "Crystal Atom notation" used consistently
