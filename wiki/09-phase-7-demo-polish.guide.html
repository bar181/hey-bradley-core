Key Takeaway

The spec roundtrip test was the single most valuable finding since Phase 1. Generating HUMAN (B+) and AISP (B−) specs from a real site config, then feeding the AISP spec to an external LLM to produce HTML, proved that Crystal Atom notation is a genuine differentiator—but only when the data is complete. Truncation at 30 chars and slice(0,4) limits in the generators were unforgivable bugs for the core innovation. Phase 7 is polish-only: no new features, just making everything work correctly.
